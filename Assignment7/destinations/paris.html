Paris, France

City, Culture Moderate

About Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light" and is one of the most visited destinations in the world. From its iconic Eiffel Tower to the artistic heritage of the Louvre, Paris offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and romance.

Why Visit Paris

Paris captivates visitors with its elegant architecture, world-class museums, charming cafés, and exceptional cuisine. Whether you're strolling along the Seine River, exploring historic neighborhoods, or indulging in pastries at a local patisserie, Paris offers unforgettable experiences at every turn.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Paris is from April to June and October to November when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller. Summer (July and August) brings warmer temperatures but also the largest crowds and some business closures as locals take their holidays.

Quick Facts

  • Language: French
  • Currency: Euro (€)
  • Time Zone: Central European Time (CET)
  • Population: 2.1 million
  • Weather: Temperate climate with mild summers and cool winters
